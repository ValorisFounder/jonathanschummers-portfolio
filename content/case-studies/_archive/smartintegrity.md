## Headline

Innovating through iteration: Creating solutions that truly make a difference

## Subtitle

Reducing the number of leaks by anticipating inspections on pipes assessed as the highest risk for leaks, through better estimation of the impact of IOWs on corrosion rate

## Context & Objectives

- Helping inspectors better estimate the corrosion rate to adjust their inspection schedules and reduce the number of leaks in the refinery.
- The idea is to simplify risk analysis for inspectors by offering a consolidated view of information on integrity/corrosion in a single tool and enabling better estimation of the impact of deviations from standard operating procedures (IOWs) on the corrosion rate.
- Previously, risk analysis was very time-consuming for inspectors as the information was scattered across different tools. Additionally, they struggled to quantify the impact of IOWs on the corrosion rate between two interventions.

## Challenges

- Adapting and building a product vision tailored to the pilot site as well as other refineries, considering their differing needs and priorities.
- Collaborating with data engineers and data scientists to identify and gather data in order to propose SMINT's differentiating feature: a better estimation of the impact of IOWs on the corrosion rate.

## Business Value

Leaks caused by corrosion lead to production shutdowns costing millions of euros per day, while also exposing on-site workers to potentially severe risks.

The goal is to increase revenue and reduce operating expenses (OPEX):
- Limit losses due to downtime related to integrity (EUR/ton)
- Reduce the percentage of inspections with no added value

## Target & Method

- 13 main users (pilot site) and 30 inspectors on future sites
- 7 user interviews
- 1 persona
- 1 "as-is" process
- Co-design workshops to design the app
- Around 30 user tests throughout the project duration
- SUS (System Usability Scale)
- Basic UX analytics with App Insight

## Product Team

- 1 Product Designer (myself)
- 1 Product Owner (PO), 1 Project Manager (PM), and 1 Scrum Master
- 1 Tech Lead, 3 Full Stack Developers, 2 Data Engineers, and 1 Data Scientist

## Impact

- 6% reduction in type 2 leaks after one year of use at the pilot site, compared to the previous three years.
- Solution deployed to 500 users across 4 sites in 3 countries.
- Inspectors: 82% DAU, 3 sessions/day per active user
- Process engineers: 65% WAU, 1.5 sessions/week per active user
- Integrated 5 tools into an Azure cloud data lake to accelerate data access for other refinery projects.
